MLBB

A multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) game designed for mobile phones. The game is free-to-play and is only monetized through in-game purchases like characters and skins. Each player can control a selectable character, called a Hero, with unique abilities and traits.

5 reasons Why I love this game:

  • It is fun.
  • Heros are unique.
  • It relives stress.
  • You can play with friends.
  • Moonton is generous now a days.
